Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Always use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
2. Always enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on accounts where it’s available.
3. Never share OTPs, passwords, and personal information with anyone.
4. Avoid clicking on unknown and suspicious links received via SMS, Email, and WhatsApp.
5. Avoid downloading apps and files from unknown sources.
6. Always verify payment requests before approving them on UPI apps.
7. Never enter UPI PIN to receive money; it’s required to send money only.
8. Always check bank statements and transaction history regularly.
9. Use caution while using public Wi-Fi for online transactions.
10. Always update devices and apps regularly to avoid security issues.

Reporting:
In case you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately at:
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
